What I Solve

Focused simulation support for the failure modes that cost molders the most time, margin, and credibility — from first DFM through T1 correlation.

DFM & Gate Optimization

Preliminary DFM analysis and Moldflow studies to determine gate count, gate location, clamp force, injection pressure, and projected cycle time before tool configuration is locked.

Warpage & Windage

Multi-solver warpage analysis (CRIMS, Uncorrected Residual Strain, STAMP) and windage model generation for glass-filled polymers exceeding GD&T tolerances.

Defect Prevention

Identify and resolve short shots, unbalanced filling, air traps, weld lines, and excessive injection / clamp forces before steel is ordered.

Cooling & Packing

Runner and cooling-line design in collaboration with hot runner suppliers, plus packing optimization for volumetric shrinkage uniformity and gate freeze time.

Structural FEA

Deflection, stress, and dimensional performance validation — critical for metal-to-plastic conversions and automotive structural compliance.

TIS 3.1 / T1 Correlation

Full Moldflow correlation studies and T1 protocol support to satisfy GM's TIS 3.1 requirements, including scan-data vs. nominal math data validation.
